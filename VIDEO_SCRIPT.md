## Tips for Recording

1. **Prepare the chat history beforehand** so responses are instant (or edit in post)
2. **Use smooth cursor movements** - avoid jerky scrolling
3. **Pause on key sections** (Journey Tracker, Confidence Meter) for 2-3 seconds
4. **Highlight disclaimers** - zoom in or use annotations
5. **Show realistic scenarios** - salary delays, consumer complaints, cybercrime
6. **Keep audio clean** - professional mic or voiceover in post-production
7. **Add background music** - subtle, not distracting
8. **Include captions** - accessibility + clarity
